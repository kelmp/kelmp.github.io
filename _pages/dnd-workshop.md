## Character Creation

### Summary

You'll need to figure out:

- Backstory
- Class
- Race
    - Racial ASIs
- Background
- Starting level
- Starting stats
    - Point buy
    - ["Instant" ASIs](#stats)

### Level

Start anywhere in levels 1-3, whatever makes most sense with your backstory.
If you pick a lower level you'll gain two levels at each milestone until you're
caught up. So:

| Start level | Milestone 1 | Milestone 2 |
|:-----------:|:-----------:|:-----------:|
|      1      |      3      |      5      |
|      2      |      4      |      5      |
|      3      |      4      |      5      |

We won't be going above level 12 to 13, tops.

### Stats

Standard point buy. In addition, you can immediately take all ASIs for
your class that occur after level 12 (fighters get 3, everyone else gets 2).
If you're multiclassing, you get all ASIs that happen after character level 12.

At most one of these "instant" ASIs can be used to take a feat.

### Races

Variant human and custom lineage are not allowed - a feat at character creation
is only doable via the instant ASIs above.

All races have the additional rules at the bottom of this section. Any changes
to races made in Monsters of the Multiverse (see 
[this thread](https://old.reddit.com/r/dndleaks/comments/s3lc5c/compilation_of_race_changes_from_from_monsters_of/)
until WotC releases the book) are in play.

#### Additional Race Rules

Most of these are from MotM.

- **Racial ASI.** Instead of the racial stat bonuses written, all races now give 
  a +2 to one stat and a +1 to a different stat.
- **Languages.** Your character knows 2 languages of your choice from their
  race, instead of whatever the racial traits say.
- **Speed.** All creatures with a walking speed of 25ft now have a speed of
  30ft. In addition, any special speeds (swimming, flying, climbing) are now all
  equal to your walking speed.
- **Innate Spellcasting.** Any trait that lets you cast a spell can be cast
  using any spell slots you have. You also get to decide whether to use
  INT, WIS, or CHA as your spellcasting stat for these spells.
- **Short Rest Traits.** All traits that refresh on a short rest now be used a
  number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus per long rest.
- **Natural Weapons.** All natural weapons that dealt 1d4 + STR damage now deal
  1d6 + STR, and count as unarmed strikes instead of "natural weapon" attacks.
- **Magical Resistance.** Traits that gave advantage on saving throws against
  spells and other magical effects now only apply to spells.

### Backgrounds

All officially released material is fine, other than backgrounds from Strixhaven
or Ravnica that grant additional spells.
